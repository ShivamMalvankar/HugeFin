How It Works

We do the hard structuring before the market conversation starts.

That discipline protects clients from weak lender fit and protects partners from incomplete, poorly framed proposals.

01

Mandate Diagnosis

We assess the project, cash flows, collateral, promoter profile, regulatory context, and urgency before recommending a capital path.

02

Capital Architecture

The funding requirement is converted into a clear structure with proposed ticket size, pricing logic, security, timelines, and exit path.

03

Partner Matching

Only aligned lenders or investors are approached, based on product appetite, risk tolerance, geography, ticket size, and speed.

04

Execution Support

HugeFin stays involved through diligence, term negotiation, sanction coordination, documentation, and final drawdown.
