How It Works

Our 4-Step Capital Advisory Process

01

Initial Discovery

We understand your project scope, stage of development, funding requirement and timeline through a structured briefing session.

02

Capital Structuring

Our advisors design the optimal funding structure - debt, equity, mezzanine or hybrid - tailored to your project and risk profile.

03

Lender Matchmaking

We present your transaction to relevant capital partners from our curated network of institutional lenders, NBFCs and private funds.

04

Execution & Closure

We support end-to-end documentation, negotiations and disbursement coordination until capital is deployed in your project.
